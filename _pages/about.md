Hello! I recently obtained my Ph.D. in mathematics from the University of Washington, where my advisors are [Kyle Ormsby](https://kyleormsby.github.io/) and [John Palmieri](https://sites.math.washington.edu//~palmieri/). This fall, I will be a postdoc at the [SLMath semester program on motivic homotopy theory](https://www.slmath.org/programs/384). Starting in January 2027, I will be a postdoc at Vanderbilt University.

My [research interests](research.md) are in motivic and chromatic homotopy theory. I am generally interested in periodicity in all flavors of stable homotopy theory.
